Frequently Asked Questions

How do I predict employee performance?

To predict employee performance, fill out the form in the "Predict Employee Performance" section on the main dashboard. Enter the required information such as age, years at company, job satisfaction, etc., and click the "Predict Performance" button.

How does the team formation feature work?

The team formation feature helps you create an optimal team based on the required skills and team size. Enter the desired team size and list the required skills (comma-separated) in the "Form Optimal Team" section, then click the "Form Team" button.

How can I change the chart type?

You can change the chart type by selecting your preferred type from the dropdown menu labeled "Chart Type" above the charts. Your selection will be saved and applied to all charts.

How do I enable or disable notifications?

You can toggle notifications on or off in the user settings panel. Click on the "User Settings" button in the navigation bar and look for the "Enable Notifications" option.
